Suicide Methods between males and females

Findings:

  • The percentage of suicides attributable to firearms for males in 2015 was 55.6%.
  • Poisoning was the most common method of suicide for females in 2015, accounting for about one-third (33.2%) of all female suicides, where firearms accounted for 30.1%.
  • For both females and males, about one in four suicides in 2015 were attributable to suffocation (includes hanging, strangulation, and suffocation).
